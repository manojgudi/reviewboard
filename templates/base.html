📋 What is ReviewBoard?

A paper review system. Submit PDFs, collect feedback from reviewers, track revisions.

📤 How to Submit a Paper for Review
  1. Click "New Ticket" in the top nav
  2. Fill in the form: Title, abstract/description, and upload your PDF
  3. Click "Create" — your paper appears on the dashboard under "Open"
  4. Close the ticket once you got all the reviews you need (ticket owners/admins can mark tickets as closed)
📝 How to Review a Paper
  1. Open a ticket from the dashboard
  2. Read the PDF in the left panel (use page prev/next or keyboard arrows ← →)
  3. Write your review: Click anywhere on the PDF to annotate, add your feedback in the text box, choose a highlight color, then click "Submit Review"
